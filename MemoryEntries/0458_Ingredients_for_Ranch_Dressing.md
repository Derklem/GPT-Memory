# Ingredients for Ranch Dressing.

_Created: 2023-03-01T19:22:41+00:00_

What are the primary ingredients to ranch dressing

The primary ingredients for ranch dressing typically include:

- Mayonnaise: This is the base of the dressing and provides a creamy texture.
- Buttermilk: This tangy dairy product is a signature flavor of ranch dressing.
- Sour cream: This adds richness and tanginess to the dressing.
- Garlic powder: This provides a subtle garlic flavor without the harshness of fresh garlic.
- Onion powder: This adds a savory and slightly sweet onion flavor to the dressing.
- Dried dill: This herb adds a distinct flavor to the dressing and is a key ingredient in ranch dressing.
- Salt: This enhances the other flavors in the dressing.
- Pepper: This adds a mild spiciness to the dressing.

Some variations of ranch dressing may also include ingredients like chives, parsley, or lemon juice for added flavor.
