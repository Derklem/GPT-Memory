What's the best career test for a 38-year-old with a BA

There are many career tests available that can be helpful for someone looking to make a career change or explore new job opportunities. Some popular options for individuals with a BA include:

1. Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI): This test assesses personality traits and can help identify careers that may be a good fit based on an individual's strengths and preferences.

2. Strong Interest Inventory: This test measures an individual's interests and can help identify careers that align with those interests.

3. The Holland Code: This test assesses an individual's skills, values, and interests and can help identify careers that align with those areas.

4. The Birkman Method: This test is a comprehensive assessment that measures an individual's interests, behaviors, and motivations, and can help identify careers that align with those areas.

Ultimately, the best career test for a 38-year-old with a BA will depend on the individual's specific needs and goals. It is recommended to speak with a career counselor or career coach to help determine the best career test for you.
