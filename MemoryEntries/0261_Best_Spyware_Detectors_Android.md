Detecting spyware on an Android device is crucial to protect your privacy and security. Several high-quality apps can help detect and remove spyware from your Android phone. Here are some of the best spyware detectors for Android:

### 1. **Malwarebytes Mobile Security**
- **Features:** Malwarebytes offers real-time protection, scans for spyware and adware, and provides a privacy audit for all installed apps.
- **Pros:** Reliable, regularly updated, easy to use, and offers a free version.
- **Cons:** Some features require a premium subscription.

### 2. **Avast Antivirus**
- **Features:** Includes anti-spyware, anti-theft, photo vault, and app lock features. It also scans Wi-Fi networks for vulnerabilities.
- **Pros:** Comprehensive protection, additional security features, and a free version.
- **Cons:** Contains ads in the free version and may consume more battery.

### 3. **Bitdefender Mobile Security**
- **Features:** Offers real-time protection, scans for malware and spyware, and provides web protection.
- **Pros:** Lightweight, high detection rates, and excellent phishing protection.
- **Cons:** Some features are only available in the premium version.

### 4. **Norton Mobile Security**
- **Features:** Provides protection against malware, spyware, and phishing. It also offers Wi-Fi security and web protection.
- **Pros:** Trusted brand, high detection rates, and comprehensive protection.
- **Cons:** Requires a subscription for full features.

### 5. **Kaspersky Mobile Antivirus**
- **Features:** Includes malware and spyware detection, app lock, and anti-theft features.
- **Pros:** Strong malware detection, user-friendly interface, and a free version available.
- **Cons:** Some features are limited to the premium version.

### 6. **Lookout Security & Antivirus**
- **Features:** Offers spyware detection, identity theft protection, and system security alerts.
- **Pros:** Provides additional identity protection features, such as breach reports.
- **Cons:** Requires a subscription for premium features.

### 7. **McAfee Mobile Security**
- **Features:** Provides anti-malware, anti-spyware, Wi-Fi security, and privacy protection features.
- **Pros:** Comprehensive protection, user-friendly interface, and additional privacy features.
- **Cons:** Can be resource-intensive and requires a premium subscription for some features.

### Tips for Choosing the Best Spyware Detector
- **Detection Rate:** Choose an app with a high detection rate for malware and spyware.
- **Real-time Protection:** Ensure the app provides real-time protection to prevent spyware infections.
- **Resource Usage:** Opt for apps that are lightweight and don’t drain your phone’s battery.
- **User Reviews:** Consider apps with positive user reviews and high ratings on the Google Play Store.

### General Tips for Protecting Your Android Device
- **Keep your OS updated**: Regular updates patch vulnerabilities.
- **Download apps from official sources**: Only use the Google Play Store or other trusted sources.
- **Be cautious with permissions**: Only grant permissions that are necessary for the app’s functionality.
- **Use a VPN**: For additional privacy and security, especially on public Wi-Fi networks.

Choosing the right spyware detector depends on your specific needs and preferences, but any of these options should offer solid protection against spyware threats.
