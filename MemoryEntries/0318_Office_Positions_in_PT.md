What is a physical therapist assistant

**assistant:**

A Physical Therapist Assistant (PTA) is a healthcare professional who works under the supervision of a licensed physical therapist. PTAs assist in providing physical therapy treatments to patients, including exercises, therapeutic activities, and other interventions. They play a crucial role in supporting patients' rehabilitation and helping them regain mobility and functionality.

How do I take a screenshot on a PC

**assistant:**

On a Windows PC, you can take a screenshot using the "Snipping Tool" or "Snip & Sketch" app, depending on your Windows version. Alternatively, you can use keyboard shortcuts:

1. **Entire Screen:**
   - Press the "PrtScn" (Print Screen) key to capture the entire screen.
   - To capture only the active window, press "Alt + PrtScn."

2. **Snipping Tool (Windows 10):**
   - Search for "Snipping Tool" in the Start menu.
   - Open the tool and choose the type of snip you want (rectangular, free-form, window, or full-screen).

3. **Snip & Sketch (Windows 10):**
   - Press "Windows + Shift + S" to open Snip & Sketch.
   - Select the area you want to capture.

4. **Windows Snip & Sketch (Windows 11):**
   - Press "PrtScn" to open Snip & Sketch directly.

After taking the screenshot, you can paste it into an image editor (like Paint) or directly into a document by pressing "Ctrl + V."
