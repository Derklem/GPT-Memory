5. **Rakuten Advertising:** Formerly known as Rakuten Marketing, this platform connects affiliates with advertisers and offers a diverse range of products and services.

6. **Impact Radius:** Impact Radius is a comprehensive marketing platform that includes affiliate marketing among its services. It provides advanced tracking and reporting features.

7. **FlexOffers:** FlexOffers is an affiliate marketing network that connects publishers with a variety of advertisers in different niches.

8. **Awin:** Awin is a global affiliate marketing network that facilitates partnerships between affiliates and merchants, providing access to a broad range of advertisers.

9. **JVZoo:** JVZoo is a digital product marketplace that focuses on internet marketing and online business products. It is popular for software, plugins, and digital courses.

10. **MaxBounty:** MaxBounty is a CPA (Cost Per Action) affiliate network that focuses on lead generation and sales. It covers various niches and offers a variety of campaigns.
