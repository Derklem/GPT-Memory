# Create business accounts: YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook

How do i create a business account on youtube, tiktok and instagram/facebook off of my personal account

**assistant:**

Creating business accounts on YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook typically involves slightly different processes for each platform. Here's a general guide for each:

### YouTube:
1. Sign in to your personal Google account.
2. Go to YouTube and click on your profile picture in the top right corner.
3. Click on "Your Channel."
4. Click on "Customize Channel."
5. Click on "Settings."
6. Under the "Channel status and features" section, click "Add or remove managers."
7. Click "Manage permissions."
8. Click on "Add" and enter the email address you want to use for your business account.
9. Choose the role for the new account (e.g., Manager or Viewer).
10. Click "Invite."

### TikTok:
1. Open the TikTok app and sign in to your personal account.
2. Go to your profile by tapping on "Me" at the bottom right.
3. Tap on the three dots in the top right corner to open settings.
4. Scroll down and tap on "Switch to Pro Account."
5. Choose the category that best fits your business.
6. Follow the prompts to set up your business account.

### Instagram:
1. Sign in to your personal Instagram account.
2. Go to your profile by tapping on your profile picture at the bottom right.
3. Tap on the three horizontal lines in the top right corner.
4. Tap on "Settings."
5. Scroll down and tap on "Account."
6. Tap on "Switch to Professional Account."
7. Choose the category for your business.
8. Connect your business Facebook page (if you have one) or create a new one.

### Facebook:
1. Sign in to your personal Facebook account.
2. Go to the top right corner and click on the down arrow, then select "Create Page."
3. Choose the type of page you want to create (e.g., Business or Brand, Community or Public Figure).
4. Fill in the required information about your business.
5. Click "Continue" and follow the prompts to set up your business page.
6. Once your page is created, you can manage it separately from your personal account.

Remember to tailor your profiles with relevant information, branding, and content to effectively represent your business on each platform.
